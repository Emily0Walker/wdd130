Undercurrent

Experience thrilling rafting adventures amidst stunning natural scenery for adrenaline junkies, family bonding, or corporate team-building. Our trips cater to all ages, ensuring safety and enjoyment for everyone onboard. Join us for unforgettable memories and exciting challenges on the river!

History

Undercurrent was founded in 2010 by a group of adventure enthusiasts passionate about sharing the exhilaration of white water rafting. Nestled along the roaring rivers of Colorado, the company quickly gained a reputation for its expertly guided tours and commitment to safety. Over the years, Undercurrent has expanded its offerings to include various levels of rapids, catering to both beginners and seasoned rafters. Today, it stands as a premier destination for thrill-seekers, known for delivering unforgettable experiences where excitement rides the undercurrent.
